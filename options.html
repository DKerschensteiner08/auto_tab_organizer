Smart Tab Tidy

Group, sort, park, and tidy your browser tabs.

AI Grouping

Cluster open tabs by topic with OpenAI embeddings. Tab titles and (optionally) page snippets are sent to OpenAI.

Stored locally in your browser. Required for AI grouping.

Higher values create tighter, more conservative groups.

Behavior

One domain per line. Subdomains are also excluded.

Automation

Runs the AI grouping action automatically when the active tab is not on an excluded domain.

  • Ctrl/⌘ Shift G Group by Domain
  • Ctrl/⌘ Shift P Park Tabs
  • Ctrl/⌘ Shift D Close Duplicates
